Internal Safety Collapse
in Frontier Large Language Models

ISC can make tested frontier LLMs produce outputs they would normally refuse
through task completion inside agentic workflows.

A single benign user instruction can start the workflow; after that, the agent reads the workspace, infers missing fields, and completes the task automatically.

ISC-Bench & The TVD Framework

How to Inspect ISC

To learn what ISC is and how it works, refer to our paper and tutorials.

  1. Read our paper and inspect the official codebase templates
  2. Review the lower-risk evidence in the linked community cases before modifying any task surface
  3. For TVD Agent, use one benign initial instruction such as "start" or "finish the workflow"; the remaining steps are automated by the agent
